Welcome to Red Cliffs Cavaliers!
We are dedicated to raising happy, healthy, and loving puppies right in the heart of our home. Each of our Cavaliers is part of our daily life, ensuring that every puppy is well socialized and comfortable around people of all ages. We take great pride in responsible breeding practices. All of our parent dogs are carefully selected and fully health tested to give our puppies the very best start in life.
Whether you’re looking for a loyal companion, a gentle family dog, or a cuddly new best friend, our puppies are raised with love, care, and attention every step of the way.
Thank you for visiting! We are excited to help you find your perfect Cavalier!
